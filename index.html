Ica-Perú

Ica is located in the southern coast of Peru and is known as the "Land of the Eternal Sun" due to its warm, desert climate all year round. The region is famous for its unique geography, featuring the spectacular Huacachina Oasis, the only natural desert oasis in South America, surrounded by massive sand dunes. Culturally, Ica is the heart of Peru's wine and Pisco production, rich in Afro-Peruvian folklore, and steeped in ancient history as the home of the pre-Inca Paracas and Nazca civilizations.
