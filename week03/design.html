Applying Visual Hierarchy & Typography

Visual Hierarchy

Visual hierarchy is the arrangement or presentation of elements in a way that implies importance. In other words, it influences the order in which the human eye perceives what it sees. This order is created by the visual contrast between forms in a field of perception.

By using size, color, contrast, alignment, and repetition, you can guide a user's attention to the most critical information first, creating a seamless and intuitive user experience.

Pro Tip!

Always ensure your callout boxes have sufficient color contrast so they are readable by everyone.
